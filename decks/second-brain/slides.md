<div class="section-header">
  <h1>The PARA Method</h1>
</div>

<p class="dim" style="margin-top: 0.5rem; margin-bottom: 1.5rem;">by Tiago Forte — this is what changed how I think about organization</p>

<div class="story">
  <p>The idea is simple: <strong>stop deciding where things go in the moment.</strong></p>
  <p style="margin-top: 1rem;">Everything goes into an <span class="accent"><strong>Inbox</strong></span> first. Then, when you have time, you move things to one of four places:</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-top: 1.5rem; display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(4,1fr); gap: 1.5rem; text-align: center;">
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 700; color: #0d9488;">P</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Projects</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Active work</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 700; color: #0d9488;">A</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Areas</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Responsibilities</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 700; color: #0d9488;">R</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Resources</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Reference material</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 2rem; font-weight: 700; color: #0d9488;">A</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Archive</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Done</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!--
"Four categories. That's it. The magic is that you capture first and organize later. You're never stuck deciding where something goes while you're in the middle of something else."

<div class="section-header">
  <h1>My story</h1>
</div>

<div class="story" style="margin-top: 1.25rem;">
  <p>Before, my notes lived in six different places. Notion for work, Chrome bookmarks for articles, WhatsApp for things friends shared, Apple Notes for random ideas, Google Docs for actual documents, Slack's saved items for things I meant to follow up on.</p>

  <p style="margin-top: 1rem;">I saved everything and found nothing.</p>
</div>

<div class="callout" style="display: flex; gap: 1.5rem; align-items: center;">
  <div style="flex: 1;">I tried PARA. It helped. But after a while I was still overthinking it — "Is this an Area or a Resource?" I once spent 11 minutes deciding where to put a pasta recipe. Eleven.</div>

        <p style="font-weight: 700; font-size: 1rem;">📥 Inbox</p>

      <div>

<div class="section-header">
  <h1>Where AI comes in</h1>
</div>

<div class="story" style="margin-top: 1.25rem;">
  <p>The hardest part of any system is keeping it up. Life gets busy, you stop filing things, and it falls apart.</p>
  <p style="margin-top: 1rem;">AI doesn't replace the system. It makes the system <span class="accent"><strong>frictionless</strong></span>.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-top: 1.5rem; display: grid; grid-template-columns: repeat(3,1fr); gap: 1rem; text-align: center;">
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 1.5rem;">📥</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.5rem;">Capture</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Summarize and save without effort</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 1.5rem;">🗂️</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.5rem;">Organize</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">File and tag automatically</p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p style="font-size: 1.5rem;">🔍</p>
    <p style="font-weight: 600; margin-top: 0.5rem;">Retrieve</p>
    <p class="dim" style="font-size: 0.9rem; margin-top: 0.25rem;">Find what you need, in context</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="section-header">
  <h1>A real example</h1>
</div>

<div class="story" style="margin-top: 1.25rem;">
  <p>Last week I had to fill a performance review form. I had one hour.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-top: 1.25rem; display: grid; grid-template-columns: 1fr 1fr; gap: 2rem;">
  <div>
    <p class="compare-label-red">Without a system</p>
    <p class="compare-text">
      I'd be digging through months of Slack messages, ClickUp history, old meeting notes — trying to remember what I actually did. Takes hours. You still miss half of it.
    </p>
  </div>
  <div>
    <p class="compare-label-green">With the system</p>
    <p class="compare-text">
      I asked my AI to help me fill the form from my notes. It pulled from my meeting notes, project logs, things I'd captured over time. Done in 20 minutes.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>
